01. about()

I operate at the intersection of enterprise software, Generative AI, data engineering, and product development — usually leading initiatives end to end, from the first whiteboard sketch to a deployed, customer-facing system.

Over the last several years at Compliance Group, I've helped build and modernize Quality, Document, and Training Management platforms for regulated industries — and more recently led the push to embed AI copilots, RAG-based knowledge systems, and agentic workflows directly into those products.

I think less like a pure developer and more like a technical product lead: weighing business impact, shaping features, and balancing technical feasibility against real customer value.

A few things I care about:

  • End-to-end ownership — requirements through deployment
  • Bringing AI into enterprise software, responsibly
  • Systems thinking: platforms, pipelines & reusable frameworks
  • Learning fast across AI, cloud, product & compliance
