How to get the most out of this

✅

Type the code, don't just read it. Every blue editor box is live — change the values and hit Run (or Ctrl+Enter). Breaking things and fixing them is how you learn.

🧪

Do the challenges. Each module has "Try it yourself" boxes. Don't skip them — they turn reading into real skill.

🧠

Take the quizzes. They're not graded — they're there to catch misunderstandings before they pile up.

🔁

It's okay to be confused. Confusion is the feeling of learning. Re-read, re-run, and move on — concepts click on the second and third pass.
