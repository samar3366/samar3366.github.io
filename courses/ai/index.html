How This Book Works

Every chapter follows the same pattern, designed so you understand before you memorize:

  1. 🧒 Story or AnalogyA relatable opener — LEGO bricks, recipes, a librarian. If a 10-year-old can picture it, you can build on it.
  2. 📖 What It Actually IsThe technical definition, in plain English. Real terminology introduced gently, then reinforced.
  3. 🎨 Pictures, Pictures, Pictures5–7 SVG diagrams per chapter. Concepts you can see stick longer than concepts you only read.
  4. 🌍 Real-World Examples"This is what happens when you ask ChatGPT…" — concrete examples from Claude, GPT, Midjourney, Sora, etc.
  5. 💼 Pro Talk BoxThe exact jargon experts use. So when you join a meeting, you can speak fluently — not just understand.
  6. 🧠 Self-Check Questions3–5 questions per chapter. Answer them out loud — that's how you know you've actually learned it.
  7. 📚 References & Going DeeperBooks, papers, courses, videos — with specific page numbers, durations, and which parts to read.
⏱ Time Commitment

Each chapter is ~30–45 minutes of focused reading. Total ~14–18 hours end-to-end. Don't rush. Re-reading is normal — concepts compound.
